## Design System

**Neon-Dark Gaming Aesthetic:**
- Primary: Electric Green (#00ff88), Electric Blue (#00ccff)
- Background: Deep dark (#0a0a0f, #1a1a24, #2a2a3a)
- Typography: JetBrains Mono (data), Inter (UI)
- Animations: Spring physics with 0.3s standard duration
- Effects: Glow shadows, gradient borders, particle systems
